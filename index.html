HOW IT WORKS

Discover You

We expect to meet you to understand your requirements, evaluate your needs, function requirements, discussing ideas to help us create a design that meets your sense of style. We share the formal quotation with you. Once we have a mutual understanding with you upon the design aspects and the operative timelines. We can start the design process.

Design Stage

In this process we request you to spend some time with our designers to share your thoughts about designs, themes and materials. Post that, our designers create the Schematic Designs and Conceptual proposal through the help of 2D elevations and 3D sketches for your approval.

Execution Stage

Our team of craftsmen and supervisors start the process of execution onsite under expert supervision to ensure there are no deviations from the approved designs. You will also get regular updates on the progress from our side. Here at Atrio Design Studio, we believe in quality so we perform thorough quality checks before the final handover.
