Welcome to Atrio Design Studio

Design & Build

Atrio Design Studio is an architectural and interior design services firm based in Bangalore specialising in high end as well as affordable residential and commercial projects. We have experience in creating functional, aesthetic and innovative design spaces ranging from residences, offices, and commercial. We have a team of passionate and highly experienced Architects and Interior Designers on a mission to bring your dream into reality.
"Our Vision is to achieve the highest possible standards of design and execution while establishing our organization as preferred architectural and interior design company."

Here at Atrio Design Studio,our objective is to develop innovative design solutions that enable ourclients to enhance their competitive advantage and achieve their business and cultural goals. We create customized designs to make sure everything fits perfectly and is truly special to each client. Our best use of cutting edge technology allows customers to visualise their spaces in real before even we commence the work.

We create spaces that truly inspire and reflect our client's personality and creativity. We provide turnkey based solution for all the architectural and interior needs from designing stage to complete execution. At Atrio Design Studio, what we promise to our customers is what they get as an end product.

Our Journey

Its everyone's dream in life to build their own house But Dreaming is easy, Reality is not since it's stressful and mostly people have no idea even how to begin. Its difficult to find the right Architects, the contractors and vendors and also getting approvals from authorities.

We, at Atrio realised the pain of the customers and started the journey to provide the world class experience who want to either architecture or interior services. We envisioned to provide all design services under one roof from Architecture Design -> Interior Design & execution and provide awesome and hassle free experience. To take further our goal we started defining the processes to bridge this gap by setting up
  • A team of highly experienced Architects/Designers who can create focused & unique designs as per the utility and architecture of the space using the latest technologies.
  • A team of trusted skilled contractors and reliable vendors
  • A team of engineers to make sure the smooth co-ordination between designers and contractors and also can efficiently handle every detail of the project with complete transparency.

Over the last 4 years, after successfully making many home owners happy, we have expanded our horizons to the Commercial Turnkey Interiors, Civil Construction and providing Architectural Designs and 3D Walkthrough & Visualisation services to various builders and architects pan India.

At Atrio Design, we drive a few objectives — Quality, Transparency, Trust and Convenience. We always give our best work so that our clients can get as what they want.
